# Codra Investor Demo Script: The Solo Agency Production Flow

**Duration:** 3 Minutes
**Persona:** Alex, a Solo Agency Founder managing 5 client brand identities.

---

### Scene 1: The Production Dashboard (0:00 - 0:45)

**Visual:** Alexander logs into the Codra Production Registry.
**Narrative:**
"Meet Alex. Alex runs a solo creative agency. He’s currently balancing five different brand identities, and his biggest pain is context switching.

In a traditional workflow, Alex would spend hours briefing freelancers or wrestling with generic AI tools that don’t remember his client's voice.

Codra system overview. This isn't just a project list; it's a **Production Registry**. Every workspace here represents a high-fidelity 'Spread'—a context-aware workspace tailored to a specific client assignment."

---

### Scene 2: Strategic Intake (0:45 - 1:30)

**Visual:** Alex initiates a 'New Client Assignment' (Onboarding).
**Narrative:**
"When Alex takes on a new project—say, a seed-round pitch for a Fintech startup—he doesn't start with a blank prompt. He starts with a **Strategic Consultation**.

Codra's onboarding is designed for pre-production. We define the 'North Star Message', the 'Strategic Narrative', and the 'Deliverables' upfront.

Notice how the tone is editorial, not technical. We're building a 'Source of Truth' that our AI will use for every single task."

---

### Scene 3: The Spread & Smart Execution (1:30 - 2:30)

**Visual:** Alex enters the 'Spread' and runs an 'Investor Deck' task.
**Narrative:**
"This is the **Spread**. It’s where the strategy meets execution.

On the left, the **TOC** shows Alex’s production backlog. On the right, the **Tear Sheet** acts as an active memory bank.

Alex needs a narrative framework for the pitch deck. He hits 'Run'. Behind the scenes, Codra’s **Smart Router** identifies this as a high-complexity creative task and routes it to Claude 3.5 Sonnet for maximum precision.

The output isn't just generic copy. It's grounded in the 'Guardrails' and 'Success Criteria' Alex defined ten minutes ago."

---

### Scene 4: Export & Delivery (2:30 - 3:00)

**Visual:** Alex views the completed Tear Sheet and clicks 'Export to PDF'.
**Narrative:**
"Production is about delivery. Alex reviews the generated context, makes minor editorial tweaks, and with one click, he has a professional **PDF Tear Sheet** ready to send to his client for approval.

What used to take an entire weekend now takes an hour. Alex isn't just working faster; he's maintaining a higher strategic standard for every client, across every brand.

That’s Codra: The Production Studio for the New Creative Economy."
